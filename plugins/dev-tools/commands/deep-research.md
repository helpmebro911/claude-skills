description: Deep research and discovery before building something new
argument-hint: "[focused|wide|deep] [topic or product idea]"
---

Load the `deep-research` skill.

Parse $ARGUMENTS for:
- **Depth**: `focused` (30 min), `wide` (1-2 hours), `deep` (3-6 hours). Default: wide
- **Topic**: what to research

Examples: `/deep-research deep obsidian replacement on cloudflare`, `/deep-research focused codemirror 6 vs prosemirror`, `/deep-research wide knowledge management tools`
